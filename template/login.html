The aim of this e-learning program is to help users in learning the topic Differentiation. The e-learning program delivers the contents of the topic on how to do the process of differentiation. Users can practice doing the differentiation through the guided exercises provided with relevant hints and feedbacks. Users can evaluate their own learning on differentiation by answering some test questions to gauge their mastery of the skills. Users' learning progress will be shown through the progress report generated by the e-learning system.
Have a wonderful time in using this e-learning for learning Differentiation.
